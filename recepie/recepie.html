Your Paneer Kohlapuri Recipe

A typical Maharastrian taste profile, with very rich,complex spice notes,served with a crispy parantha or steamed rice, along with some amti dal or kachumbar

Total time : 50-55 minutes

Ingredients

  • Coconut: 1 cup(70 gms approx), grated
  • Dry Red Chillies: 4 pieces
  • Onion: 2 finely chopped
  • Paneer: 750 gms, cut into cubes
  • Caesar Dressing

Preparation

  1. In a kadhai , dry roast grated coconut , cloves, cinnamon, whole pepper, red chillies

    Time : 25 minutes

  2. Heat oil in a kadhai and saute the onions till soft and translucent, add ginger-garlic paste and fry it till it is brown and the oil begins to seperate

    Time : 10 minutes

  3. Add the ground spices , mix well and fry for 5-10 minutes. Add water, paneer.Cook it till it becomes thick.

    Time : 20 minutes
